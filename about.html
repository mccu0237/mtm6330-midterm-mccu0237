About

I love anything having to do with the media world. It is a place where I can express my art and design. The options are infinite when it comes to creating content. The media field is constantly changing which is why it is such a great field because there is always something new. Technology is our future and there are so many ways we can be creative with it. We can inspire so many people with just a simple creation, which is something I want to be a part of. Graphic design, photography, videography, content management, and web development are all trades I have learnt over the last year and a half. Whilst none of them are easy jobs, they are all extremely interesting and allow for me to fully let my creative and technical side be used.
