Welcome!

Portraits are some of my favourite pictures to take. They are very real and offer a lot of emotion. They show the art of uniqueness between each person. They hold stories about each individual. Portraits allow the viewers to connect to something. They can feel the emotion in the picture, and they connect to how the portraits make them feel.

As for photography, it is one of my favourite things. I love being able to capture a moment in time. It can freeze any moment and it is kept like that forever. Photography is an art. It looks at the finer details that we miss in life. Whether it is a portrait or a picture of the sun setting, these are all moments that we capture and we can really look at the smaller details
